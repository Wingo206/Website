The Autonomous Laser Turret is a system that uses an algorithm to detect circles with a camera. Once a circle has been detected, servos aim a laser turret to lock onto the center of the circle.

System overview:

  • Flutter app on mobile phone takes video through the rear camera

  • Configuration on the app UI allows enabling of different stages of the detection algorithm

  • Canny edge detection algorithm is applied to find edges in the image (grayscale conversion, gaussian blur, gradient calculation, threshold and thinning)

  • Hough transform is applied to find the most likely centers for circles of various radii

  • Position of circle center in screen coordinates is sent through bluetooth to the raspberry pi

  • Raspberry pi converts into servo angles and aims the laser at the center of the circle

  • Entire process loops to continuously track the object
