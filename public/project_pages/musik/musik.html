An interactive piano learning experience designed to make the process of learning piano more intuitive by displaying falling music notes aligned with a physical keyboard with a projector.

Utilized a webcam with OpenCV detect and correct the projected image to align with real-world ArUco markers placed to localize the keyboard

Used Arduino, MIDI, and USB Serial to display a heads-up feedback display on note timing on an LED strip aligned with the piano keys
